Halloween At The Zoo

We're thrilled to announce the return of our spooktacular annual event, "Halloween at the Zoo"! Get ready for a frightfully delightful experience filled with fun and festivities for the whole family.

Date: October 31 , 2024

Time: 06:30 pm - 10:00pm

Location: The Greater Utopian Zoo

Jack-o-Lantern Contest: Unleash your creativity and participate in our Jack-o-Lantern contest! Show off your carving skills and stand a chance to win spooktacular prizes.

Halloween Treats: Indulge your sweet tooth with an array of Halloween treats available for everyone. From candy apples to spooky cookies, there's something for every taste bud.

Falcon Show: Prepare to be amazed by the majestic creatures of the sky. Our thrilling falcon show is bound to captivate audiences of all ages.

Don't miss out on this unforgettable night of Halloween magic at the zoo. Gather your friends and family for an evening of laughter, surprises, and the joy of the season.

We can't wait to see you there in your spookiest costumes! Get ready for a howling good time!
